About this blog

I've always enjoyed role playing games for as long as I can remember. I don't think there is any specific reason for this facination for the genre. If I would nail it down to one reason I would say it is because there is always a level of freedom that iss nowhere to be found in most genres. When creating this blog I felt it was best to focus on the role playing genre in order to stand out from other gaming bloggers. I will provide my thoughts on past and current role playing games as well as the latest news in the gaming world. I will also post the occassional guide for the hardest sections of any role playing game. I hope this blog will become an endearing experience for all who visit.

About Me

I'm Ryan Hetherington and I'm a blogger with an interest for sharing my passion for gaming.

In 2009 I graduated from high school and since then, I've been looking for a career anywhere I could. I've been in and out of college and manual labor for the past decade. Recently I took to learning web development as it seemed fun and working from home has its advantages. I decided to dedicate myself towards blogging and combine it with my personal interests. I hope that the skills I learn pay off in the future, and I hope that I can deliver the best content for gamers everywhere.
